Licensing a Training Centre

Service Description

This service covers all applications related to the licensing of training centres. Each card below represents a separate application type — select the one that matches your situation.

License Training Center

Apply for a new licence to establish and operate a training centre in the approved emirates. The Ministry will verify that your centre meets all required standards before the licence is issued.

Service Time
10 working days
Fee
5,000 AED
Required Documents
  • ✓Commercial license of the Training Center
  • ✓Authorized Representative Passport or ID
  • ✓Authorized Representative Introductory Statement
  • ✓Certificate of Reservation of a Trade Name
Start service

Modify a Training Center License

Request changes to an existing training centre licence — such as updating ownership details, manager information, or trade name. Documents required vary depending on the type of modification.

Service Time
4 working days
Fee
2,000 AED
per modification
Required Documents
  • ✓Certificate of Reservation of a Trade Name
  • ✓Appendix to the contract signed (Owner / License Holder)
  • ✓Trade License of the Owner / License Holder
  • ✓Passport or valid ID (Owner / License Holder)
  • ✓Introductory statement (Owner / License Holder)
  • ✓New Manager CV
  • ✓Certified academic qualification (New Manager)
  • ✓Passport or valid ID (New Manager)
  • ✓New Manager Photo
Documents vary depending on the type of amendment.
Start service

Renew Training Center License

Renew an expiring training centre licence to continue operations without interruption. No additional documents are required at the time of submission.

Service Time
4 working days
Fee
5,000 AED
Required Documents
No documents required
Start service

Cancel Training Center License

Submit a formal request to cancel an active training centre licence. No fee is charged for this application type.

Service Time
2 working days
Fee
N/A
Required Documents
No documents required
Start service

Approval for Announcement

Obtain Ministry approval before publishing any advertisement or promotional material related to your training centre. Each advertisement requires a separate application and fee.

Service Time
1 working day
Fee
50 AED
per advertisement
Required Documents
  • ✓Administration-Approved Advertisement
Start service

Steps — apply to all application types

  1. 1Start the application by clicking ‘Start service’.
  2. 2Sign in via UAE Pass. If not yet registered, sign up using your Emirates ID.
  3. 3Submit the required documents relevant to your application type.
  4. 4Complete payment (if applicable) and submit application.
  5. 5Ministry reviews application.
  6. 6Applicant receives outcome.

Conditions — apply to all application types

  • 1Submit all required documents as per the initial approval document before the initial approval expires.
  • 2If there are missing documents, the customer must submit them within a maximum of five (5) working days. If not provided, the application will be closed automatically.
  • 3The training centre must continue to meet all Ministry standards throughout the licence period.
Payment channels
Payments can be made online via credit card or debit card through the Ministry's e-services portal.

Frequently asked questions

Which emirates does this service cover?
This service applies to training centres operating in Ajman, Um Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, and Fujairah.
Do documents for the Modify service vary by amendment type?
Yes. The list shown represents the full possible set of documents. The specific documents required depend on the nature of the modification being requested.
