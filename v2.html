Licensing a Training Centre

This service covers all applications related to the licensing of training centres operating under the Ministry of Higher Education and Scientific Research. Select the application type that matches your situation — each has its own steps, fees, and document requirements.

Steps — apply to all application types

  1. 1Start the application by clicking ‘Start service’.
  2. 2Sign in via UAE Pass. If not yet registered, sign up using your Emirates ID.
  3. 3Submit the required documents relevant to your application type.
  4. 4The Ministry will review your application. A payment link will be sent via email where applicable.
  5. 5Complete payment of the applicable service fee.
  6. 6Receive your licence, approval, or confirmation upon successful review.

Conditions — apply to all application types

  • 1 Submit all required documents as per the initial approval document before the initial approval expires.
  • 2 If there are missing documents, the customer must submit them within a maximum of five (5) working days. If the required documents are not provided within this period, the application will be closed automatically.
  • 3 The training centre must continue to meet all Ministry standards throughout the licence period. Failure to comply may result in suspension or cancellation of the licence.

Frequently asked questions

Which emirates does this service cover?
This service applies to training centres operating in Ajman, Um Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, and Fujairah.
What is the difference between a Licence Amendment and a Branch Addition?
An amendment covers changes to the existing licence details (e.g. name, ownership). A branch addition is a separate application for licensing a new physical location under the same entity.
Can I submit documents in stages?
Initial and final documents are submitted in two stages for the New Licence and Branch Addition. For other application types all documents are submitted at once.
