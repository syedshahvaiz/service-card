Renewal of programme accreditation

Renewal of programme accreditation

Use this service to renew the accreditation of your institution's academic programmes. The renewal process covers the review of initial programme groupings, submission of corrective action plans, and the scheduling of on-site visits.

The application is completed through a set of guided steps. You may save your progress at any time and return later to complete your submission.

Steps
  1. 1 View initial grouping of renewal of programme accreditations
  2. 2 Submit preliminary / outstanding corrective action plans
  3. 3 Select on-site visit slots
  4. 4 Review your application
  5. 5 Fee payment
  6. 6 Submit application
Conditions
  • ✓ Your programme must hold a current accreditation that is expiring within 5 months or has recently expired.
  • ✓ The renewal application must be submitted within the notification window. If the deadline is missed, the institution must contact MoHESR to request an extension.
  • ✓ All required documents must be uploaded in PDF or Word format before the application can be submitted.
  • ✓ The applicable service fee must be paid in full, or the institution must hold a valid fee exemption, before the on-site visit is scheduled.
Required documents
Corrective action plans
  • ✓ Previous full corrective action plan — most up-to-date version to be submitted.
  • ✓ Preliminary corrective action plan — required for those KPIs which score low but currently do not have a full corrective action plan.
