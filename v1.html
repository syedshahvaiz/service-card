About this application type

Apply for a new licence to establish and operate a training centre in the approved emirates. The Ministry will verify that your centre meets all required standards before the licence is issued.

☷ Required Documents
  • ✓Commercial license of the Training Center
  • ✓Authorized Representative Passport or ID
  • ✓Authorized Representative Introductory Statement
  • ✓Certificate of Reservation of a Trade Name
ⓘ Conditions
  • 1Submit all required documents before initial approval expires.
  • 2Missing documents must be submitted within 5 working days or the application is auto-closed.
  • 3Centre must meet all Ministry standards throughout the licence period.
☖ Who Can Apply

Training centre owners and authorized representatives applying to establish a new centre in the approved emirates (Ajman, Um Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah).

Frequently asked questions
Which emirates does this service cover?
This service applies to training centres operating in Ajman, Um Al Quwain, Ras Al Khaimah, and Fujairah.
What is the difference between a Licence Amendment and a Branch Addition?
An amendment covers changes to the existing licence details (e.g. name, ownership). A branch addition is a separate application for licensing a new physical location under the same entity.
Can I submit documents in stages?
Initial and final documents are submitted in two stages for the New Licence and Branch Addition. For other application types all documents are submitted at once.
